Galois Cohomology II

April 3, 2023

    During the summer term in 2023, together with Marius Leonhardt I’m creating the exercises and TAing the course Galois Cohomology II held by Dr. Alessandro Cobbe.
    The exercise sheets will be accessible through the relevant Mampf course on Thursdays, and will be discussed in the exercise session of the subsequent week:

    • When? Wednesday, 2:00 pm
    • Where? SR6
    • Contact: tholzschuh@mathi.uni-heidelberg.de

    You won’t have to hand in written solutions to the exercises, but I strongly encourage you to spend a fair amount of time thinking about them in order to get the most out of the exercise sessions.
